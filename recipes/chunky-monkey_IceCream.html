Healthy Chunky Monkey Ice Cream

Description:

A healthy banana-based icecream that is sure to please.

Ingredients:

  • 2 bananas, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon 1% milk
  • 1 tablespoon chunky peanut butter
  • 1/2 ounce dark chocolate (such as Ghiradelli), chopped

Steps:

  1. Place chopped bananas in freezer until frozen, 8 hours to overnight.
  2. Blend frozen bananas, milk, peanut butter, and chocolate in a blender until smooth.
